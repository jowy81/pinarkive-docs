# Clusters

Pinarkive organizes pinning by **clusters** — regional or logical groups that determine where your content is stored and which gateway you use to serve it.

## Available clusters

| Cluster ID | Region | Description |
|------------|--------|-------------|
| **cl0-global** | Global | Default; replicated across regions |
| **cl1-eu** | Europe | EU-based storage |
| **cl1-na** | North America | North America |
| **cl2-es** | Spain | Spain region |
| **cl2-us** | United States | US region |
| **cl2-de** | Germany | Germany region |

You can target a cluster when uploading or pinning via the API (e.g. form/body field `cl`). Your allowed clusters are returned by [GET /users/me/clusters](/api/get-clusters).

## Gateway rules

Each cluster maps to one or more gateways. Use the gateway URL that matches your cluster for best performance.

### global

| Role | URL |
|------|-----|
| **Primary** | `https://gateway.pinarkive.com/ipfs/<cid>` |
| **Secondary** | `https://ipfs.io/ipfs/<cid>` |

### eu

| Role | URL |
|------|-----|
| **Gateway** | `https://eu1.gateway.pinarkive.com/ipfs/<cid>` |

### na

| Role | URL |
|------|-----|
| **Gateway** | `https://na1.gateway.pinarkive.com/ipfs/<cid>` |

### us

| Role | URL |
|------|-----|
| **Gateway** | `https://us1.gateway.pinarkive.com/ipfs/<cid>` |

### de

| Role | URL |
|------|-----|
| **Gateway** | `https://de1.gateway.pinarkive.com/ipfs/<cid>` |

Replace `<cid>` with your content’s CID (e.g. from the upload or pin response).

## Choosing a cluster

- Use **global** for default behavior and broad replication.
- Use **eu**, **na**, **us**, or **de** when you need data in a specific region (latency or compliance).

See [Gateways](/concepts/gateways) for more on serving content and [POST /files](/api/post-files) / [POST /pin](/api/post-pin) for how to set `cl` on upload or pin.
